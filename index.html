Thirushan's Movie Suggestions for Bianca!

Animations

  • Ratatouille
    A rat named Remy dreams of becoming a great French chef despite his family's wishes.
  • Toy Story
    A story about the secret life of toys when people are not around.
  • Monsters Inc
    Monsters generate their city's power by scaring children, but they are terribly afraid of being contaminated by them.

Comedies

  • The Hangover
    Three buddies wake up from a bachelor party in Las Vegas, with no memory of the previous night and the bachelor missing.
  • Grown Ups 1 or 2
    They used to play for the same basketball team at school. Now their former coach has died, and they are reunited at his funeral. Will the group rediscover old bonds?

Dystopian

  • Maze Runner
    A young man wakes up in a giant maze with no memory of his past and must navigate it to find a way out.
  • Divergent
    In a world divided by factions based on virtues, Tris learns she's Divergent and won't fit in.

New Rom Coms

  • Lovebirds 69%
    A couple experiences a defining moment in their relationship when they are unintentionally embroiled in a murder mystery.
  • Always Be My Maybe 89%
    Childhood friends have a falling out and don't speak for 15 years, only reconnecting later in life.
  • Game Night 85%
    A group of friends' game night turns into a real-life mystery when the host is kidnapped.
  • Crazy Stupid Love 80%
    A middle-aged husband's life changes dramatically when his wife asks him for a divorce, and he seeks to rediscover his manhood with the help of a newfound friend.
